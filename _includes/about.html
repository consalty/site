Welcome,

I made these One Pagers to capture learnings from years of working as a consultant and expat. I enjoyed making “best practices” and “lessons learned” one-pages for myself as references, but figured others could find these useful too.

My goal is that some young professional out there will realize they’re not alone, and that figuring out how to be a good consultant, project manager, (or manage your time) isn’t as easy as your asshole middle manager or boss might say.

I hope this helps in some small way..
